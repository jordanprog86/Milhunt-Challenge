# Milhunt-Challenge
A Simple C++ Board Game
## History 
In a village called KBAN in the WEST many children suffer from diseases due to the lack of milk.
Their parents do not have enough money to buy them milk nor feed them in order to save their health.Two teenagers
called pod and kondy, comming from the North, arrives to KBAN and perceive the bad situation of the 
inhabitants, especially children. They were in fact searching for the Milk Mountains , which appears to 
be located inside the forests of KBAN. That mountain includes lot of treasures, and especially the Magic Milk
Bottle than can cure any disease. Pod and Kondy decided to search for that mountain, in order to get the Magic milk and
save the children. On their road to the mountain, they face many dificulties.Help them collect the Magic milk to save the children.
